About Us

Welcome to Power Pac Inc., where the variety of powersports products is second to none. We are family-owned and operated and conveniently located in Marshfield, WI.


Power Pac Inc. had its beginning in 1963 when Sid Trulen opened his business selling Scorpion Snowmobiles.


We carry many different brands of motorsports and power sports. Polaris, Kawasaki, Suzuki, Yamaha, John Deere, STIHL, and Honda Power are a few of our main brands. We also have an outstanding service department that can work on a machine of almost any brand. Our parts counter also can hook you up with the parts you need for any time of engine. Can't make it to our store? Our mobile service team can come to you! We offer pick up and deliveries as well for those with a busy lifestyle. If your in town, come check us out! We're located at the south end of town right on Central Ave.
